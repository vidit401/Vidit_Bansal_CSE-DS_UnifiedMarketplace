About Unified Marketplace

Our Mission

At Unified Marketplace, we believe shopping should be simple, transparent, and efficient. Our mission is to bring together products from across the internet, allowing you to compare prices and find the best deals without jumping between multiple websites.

What We Offer

Unified Search

Search once and see results from Amazon, Walmart, Best Buy, Target, and dozens of other retailers.

Price Comparison

Easily compare prices across different stores to make sure you're getting the best deal.

Advanced Filtering

Filter products by price, condition, store, and rating to find exactly what you're looking for.

User Accounts

Create an account to save your searches and get notified about price drops on products you're interested in.

Our Story

Unified Marketplace was founded in 2023 with a simple idea: shopping online should be easier. With so many online stores and shopping platforms, finding the best deal often meant opening dozens of tabs and manually comparing prices.

We built Unified Marketplace to solve this problem. By connecting to multiple online retailers through their APIs, we're able to bring all their products into one search interface. This means you can search once and see results from all your favorite stores, saving you time and helping you find the best deals.

Our platform continues to grow as we add more stores and improve our search capabilities. We're committed to creating the best shopping experience possible, making it easier than ever to find what you're looking for at the best price.
